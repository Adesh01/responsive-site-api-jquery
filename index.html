Six Issues to be aware of

Garbage & Plastic

Our Desire
for more 'stuff' has led to our old stuff being thrown away at an alarming and unnecessary rate

Polar Ice Cap Melt

Temperatures
in the Arctic region are rising twice as fast they are elsewhere which in turn causes rising sea levels

Over Population

Statistics
show that we have enough food to feed everyone on the planet but we end up wasting so much that others go without

Deforestation

Less Trees,
and the displacement of the wildlife, deforestation means a decrease in a natural fighter of global warming

Loss of Biodiversity

Each Species
has a role in our planet, and when one dies out, it can cause major problems for the human race

Climate Change

CO2 Levels
in the atmosphere are higher than any time before largely due to fossil fuel emissions
